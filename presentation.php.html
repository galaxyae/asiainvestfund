Presentation

With us at ASIA INVESTMENT FUND, carry out your projects with complete flexibility!

Founded in 2001 as one of the first large Asian investment companies, Asia Investment Fund focuses on different types of investment projects: construction, real estate, industry, healthcare, renewable energy, etc.

Asia Investment Fund makes equity investments in various industries, pursuing the interests of shareholders, clients and partners. Asia Investment Fund focuses on building stable competitive enterprises, implementing business models effective entrepreneurship and the creation of new enterprises.

The success of Asia Investment Fund is based on a clear competitive advantage. Asia Investment Fund has created a team of highly professional investment managers, capable of effectively managing companies in various industries, with in-depth knowledge of the specifics of international markets and the ability to react quickly and appropriately to changes in those markets, and are able to establish mutually beneficial relationships with customers.

Ibrahim Abu Mirna
